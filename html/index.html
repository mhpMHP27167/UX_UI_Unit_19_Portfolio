About Me

I am a UX Designer with a background in Visual Arts and Communications. I am both creative and analytical. I graduated from the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of Arts degree in History, a Studio Art Minor, and a Museum Studies Certificate in December of 2019. My background as a historian makes me an excellent researcher, writer, and presenter. I graduated from the Georgia Tech UX/UI boot camp in September of 2020. I worked alongside a team to design two original mobile applications and a responsive web design website. Some transferable skills to UX from previous roles include time management, editing, and collaboration.
